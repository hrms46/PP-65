About Sarathy Group

Leading the automotive industry with innovation and excellence

Our Journey in the Automotive Industry

Sarathy Group is a progressive automotive company with a strong presence across South India and expanding global aspirations. Since our establishment, we have remained committed to delivering exceptional customer service and innovative mobility solutions across a wide range of automotive segments."With a growing footprint across domestic and international markets, Sarathy Group is steadily strengthening its reputation as a trusted and forward-thinking leader in the automotive industry.", Sarathy Group continues to build its reputation as a trusted and forward-thinking industry leader.

Our unwavering dedication to excellence, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency has positioned us among the most respected and competitive automotive enterprises in the region. We are focused on delivering exceptional value to our customers, partners, and employees through ethical practices, advanced technology, and service excellence.

Furthermore, since 2012 Sarathy Automotive has been the exclusive distributor of Bajaj commercial spare parts in North Kerala, leveraging efficient logistics for 24-hour delivery.

Our Companies

Diverse portfolio of automotive businesses

SARATHY MOTORS

SARATHY MOTORS Established in 1987 with extensive operations across South Kerala, India. Specializes in providing comprehensive automotive solutions with a strong focus on Maruti Suzuki vehicles and multi-brand two-wheelers including KTM, Bajaj, Triumph, and Ather..

SARATHY BIKES

SARATHY BIKES Specializing in two‑wheeler sales and services under the Sarathy brand, with a focus on Bajaj, KTM, Triumph, Husqvarna, and Chetak, operating across multiple locations in Kollam and surrounding areas..

SARATHY ELECTRIC BIKES

Established to meet the growing demand for sustainable mobility, Sarathy Electric Bikes offers premium electric two-wheelers under the Bajaj Chetak and Ather brands. Operating across key locations in South Kerala, they provide end-to-end EV solutions including sales, service, charging support, and financing.

SARATHY AUTO CARS

Part of the Sarathy Group network, established in 1999, Sarathy Autocars is a trusted Maruti Suzuki dealership in Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram, offering a full range of sales, service, and certified pre-owned vehicles

SARATHY CARS

Incorporated in August 2016, Sarathy Cars Pvt Ltd serves as the company behind Sarathy NEXA and Arena dealerships, operating under the Sarathy Group in Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram

SARATHY AUTODRIVES

Founded in 1999, Sarathy Autodrives is the dedicated Maruti Suzuki ARENA (and True Value pre‑owned) dealership under the Sarathy Group, offering a full spectrum of sales, service, and certified used car solutions across Kollam and Thiruvananthapuram
